Factuality Channel Welcome Message WORKFLOW
6:00 PM
This is the home for the various Factuality projects (including Achilles, Patroclus and
Antimony). If you see any of those names and "Factual" or "Factuality" in a title, this is the
slack channel to talk about them. This is not the channel for general Achilles work, but if
you do have a factuality question related to another Achilles project, it's probably fine.
There is a lot of "fact-checking" in a lot of our projects, and we hope you're always
keeping a sharp eye out, but these are the projects where you are really bringing your
research A-game: thinking through claims in responses, really digging into research if
need be, and catching those crazy corner edge cases. This is a place to talk about what a
"claim" is, what might prove or disprove something, whether something is actually
Unsupported or Disputed, and the like. We're here to help, and as you all get more
exposure to these projects, we hope you can all be resources and assistance to each
other.
THINGS TO KNOW:
1. There are some useful messages that we ask you to read in the PINNED POSTS
(linked at the top left of the message window).
2. Be sure you've read the Factuality Instructions and the Achilles FAQ thoroughly! A
lot of questions are answered there. If something isn't clear there, be clear on which
part of the documentation you're asking about.
3. If you are going to ask a question about a specific work item, please give the exact
text (either in screenshot or copy paste), AND give the project ID + task ID. The devil
is always in the details; we can't answer general questions very well.
